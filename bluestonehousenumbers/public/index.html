Bluestone House Numbers

Hand-carved bluestone house numbers are a testament to the enduring beauty of natural stone and the artistry of skilled craftsmen. Each number is meticulously carved from solid bluestone, a durable and weather-resistant material.

These handcrafted numbers not only serve as practical address markers but also elevate the aesthetic appeal of any home, adding a touch of timeless elegance and individuality.

Process

To create a stone carving, a wooden template is first created to serve as a guide for the design.

Using pneumatic tools, the artist meticulously carves the design into the stone, bringing the artwork to life.

Short video of carving of number. Warning - it’s loud :)

Dimensions

Each marker is made from a 2"x14"x60" bluestone stair tread. These weigh approximately 140lbs and need to be sunk into the ground at least 16", but 20" is better. Final installed height then is 40" to 44".

Lighting

If your site has enough sunlight a solar powered spotlight works well. These provide a few hours of illumination after dusk. Homedepot sells one that is not too bright and has a warm color. It’s used for the “59” marker at night.

Pricing
